The SmartCalc v2.0 program

In the calculator, on the "Calculator" tab, you can enter any correct arithmetic expression.

Entering arithmetic expressions is carried out in the formula field by clicking on the graphic buttons. Clicking on the graphic buttons is carried out using the "computer mouse" and its left button. In an arithmetic expression, you can use brackets, integer and real decimal numbers, the variable "X", available operations and functions.

The correct arithmetic expression is calculated after pressing the "=" button. The calculation result is displayed in a special field for the result. If the calculation result is a number, then by pressing the "ans" button, you can use this result to enter the correct arithmetic expression.

Clearing the fields of the formula and the result is carried out by pressing the "C" button. The first click clears the formula field. The second press clears the result field.

On the "Function" tab, you can see a graphical representation of the entered formula on the plane. In the upper part of the tab, you can change the scope and values ​​of the function that are viewed graphically. In the same place, in the upper part, the scale of the grid along the axes is displayed.
